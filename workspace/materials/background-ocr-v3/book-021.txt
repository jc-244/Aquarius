B.3-2 The Exponentially Varying Sinusoid

We now discuss sketching an exponentially varying sinusoid

$$
f(t)=Ae^{-at}\cos(\omega_0 t+\theta)
$$

Let us consider a specific example

$$
f(t)=4e^{-2t}\cos(6t-60^\circ) \qquad \text{(B.26)}
$$

We shall sketch $4e^{-2t}$ and $\cos(6t-60^\circ)$ separately and then multiply them.
